    World Wide Web came into being by Sir Timothy John-Berners Lee
  • Timothy John "Tim" Berners-Lee, a British engineer and computer scientist, is credited with inventing the World Wide Web. He proposed the idea in 1989 and implemented the first successful HTTP client-server communication in 1990. Berners-Lee is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium, founder of the World Wide Web Foundation, and a senior researcher at MIT. He was elected to the United States National Academy of Sciences in 2009.
