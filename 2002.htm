21st Century (2002)
    Satellite Radio is launched
  • The Soviet Union launched Sputnik, the first artificial Earth satellite, on October 4, 1957, marking the beginning of the space age. Sputnik 1, a capsule, reached an Earth orbit of 940 km and 230 km, causing a "space race" between the US and Soviet Union. Sputnik 2, the first living creature to orbit Earth, carried Laika, a stray dog. Sputnik 3, the heaviest satellite, carried 12 instruments to study Earth's upper atmosphere and space.
