21st Century (2007)
    Tumblr
  • Tumblr, founded in 2007 by David Karp, is a popular social media network with over 556 million blogs. Owned by Yahoo, Verizon, and Automattic Inc., it offers a platform for creative expression and has been acquired by WordPress.com, WooCommerce, and Jetpack. The acquisition aims to increase the platform's offerings for businesses seeking unique online presence. Tumblr now allows customers to create professional websites, e-commerce stores, blogs, portfolios, and more through WooCommerce integration. Tumblr is also partnering with businesses globally to reach new audiences. With its easy drag-and-drop features and sophisticated analytics tools, Tumblr is well-positioned for a bright future.
