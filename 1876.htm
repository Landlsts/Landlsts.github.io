Industrial Age (1876)
    Invention of Telephone by Alexander Graham Bell
  • Alexander Graham Bell triumphantly secured the rights to the telephone discovery on March 7, 1876, by successfully receiving a patent for the device. Shortly after, he demonstrated its potential by making the first-ever telephone call to his partner, Thomas Watson. The telephone was a groundbreaking invention that transformed communication by enabling conversations between people in different locations.
